MBA level case-based course focused on evaluation of new venture investment opportunities from the perspective of the investor. Self-authored cases on current and former VC deals are utilized to examine the process by which VCs evaluate and manage their portfolio company investments, with participation by the VCs who originally sourced and evaluated the deals.
